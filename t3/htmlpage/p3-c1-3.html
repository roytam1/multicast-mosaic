Real-Time Multimedia on the Internet is Hot!

We have a convergence of several factors:
  • Rapidly increasing net bandwidth, pushed by Web

  • IP Multicast deployment in the MBone

  • Software implementations of H.261, MPEG, etc.

  • Processors fast enough to run them (RISC, 100MHz Pentium)

  • RTP ready to provide a standard basis
