Where We Are, Where We're Going

Current status: Proprietary protocols for audio/video (RealAudio, Internet telephones), mostly unicast with replication
  • Necessary step to forge a new industry

  • Focus on the low data rates for modem dialup
Now need standards like RTP for industry to really take off
  • Parallels the example of FAX

  • Access bandwidth and new capabilities will grow over time

  • Other layers needed in addition to RTP:

    • Resource management in the network (RSVP)

    • Session/connection management (IETF MMUSIC WG)
